A filmmaker recalls his childhood when falling in love with the pictures at the cinema of his home village and forms a deep friendship with the cinema's projectionist. lion of the desert

''Lion of the Desert'' is the story of Omar Mukhtar, the Bedouin leader who fought a brilliant, relentless guerrilla war against the Italian invaders of Libya from 1911 until his capture and execution by Mussolini's forces in 1931.
The story based on facts and true events. The action and directing of battles is top notch. Music will stay in mind forever.
